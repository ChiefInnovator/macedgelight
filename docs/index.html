Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Adjustable Glow

Control brightness, color temperature, and border width to match your mood or setup.

Bloom Mode

Dramatic outer glow for a cinematic look that really pops on camera.

Cursor Spotlight

A subtle cutout follows your cursor, making it easy to track during demos and presentations.

Screen Capture Toggle

Show the glow in OBS and screen recordings, or keep it invisible. One click.

XDR Brightness Boost

Push your display into extended dynamic range for maximum brightness on supported screens.

Auto Menu Bar

The glow intelligently hides when your cursor reaches the menu bar, then returns.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Toggle the light, adjust brightness up and down — all without leaving the keyboard.

Magnifier Loupe

A floating magnifier follows your cursor for zooming into fine details during presentations.

Hide Desktop Icons

One click to declutter your desktop for a clean presentation or recording setup.
